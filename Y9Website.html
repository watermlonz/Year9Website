UN Sustainable Development Goal 6 & 14

Clean Water and Sanitation & Life Below Water

IN 2017,

More than a third
of the world still didn't have access to clean water.

This is mostly due to three major forms of pollution:

Plastic Pollution, Sewage Pollution, & Chemical Pollution.

However, this pollution doesn't only affect humans.
It also affects Life Below Water.
